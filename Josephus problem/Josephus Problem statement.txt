Josephus Problem

There are 100 people standing in a circle in an order 1 to 100. No. 1 has a sword. He kills the next person (i.e. No. 2) and gives the sword to the next (i.e. No. 3). All people do the same until only 1 survives. Which number survives at the last?
There are 100 people starting from 1 to 100.


Input:
    1 2 3 4 5 6 
Output:
    The people who are standing in a circle : [1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6]
    The Survived people after 1st round:  [1, 3, 5]
    The Survived people after 2nd round:  [1, 5]
    The Survived people after 3rd round:  [5]
